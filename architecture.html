7-Step Hybrid Algorithm — _calculate_hybrid_matches():525-767

1
Multi-Vector Base Score
BASE = (industry × 0.25) + (stage × 0.20) + (goal × 0.20) + (geo × 0.15) + (engagement × 0.10) + (dealbreakers × 0.10)
2
Intent Classification (Line 587-598)
Detect: INVESTOR↔FOUNDER, MENTOR↔MENTEE, COFOUNDER↔COFOUNDER
3
Complementarity Score — _get_intent_quality():769-816
INVESTOR↔FOUNDER = 1.0 | MENTOR↔MENTEE = 1.0 | COFOUNDER↔COFOUNDER = 0.8 | SAME_TYPE = 0.3
4
Dealbreaker Filters (Line 651-667)
Hard exclusion: Same-objective blocking, explicit dealbreakers
5
Bidirectional Check (Line 669-686)
bidirectional_factor = sqrt(forward × reverse) / base_score, capped [0.5, 1.5]
6
Activity & Temporal Boosts (Line 688-692)
activity_boost: 0-1 based on logins | temporal_boost: 1.0→0.85 decay over 30 days
7
Final Score Formula (Line 696-702)
FINAL = BASE × (0.7 + 0.3 × intent) × bidir × (0.9 + 0.1 × activity) × (0.95 + 0.05 × temporal)
